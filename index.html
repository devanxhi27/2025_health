Welcome to Your Professional ENT Clinic

Dr. Nikolina Nikolla is an otorhinolaryngologist, graduated from the University of Medicine in Tirana.

She performs aesthetic surgeries, including rhinoplasty, blepharoplasty, and otoplasty, as well as a wide range of procedures in the field of ENT, including tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy, and septoplasty.

Surgeries

Rhinoplasty

Rhinoplasty, commonly known as nose reshaping, refines the shape and structure of the nose for improved facial balance and, when needed, better breathing. Whether performed for aesthetic reasons, functional concerns, or both, Dr. Nikolla tailors each procedure to the unique features and goals of every patient.

Otoplasty

Otoplasty corrects the shape, size, or position of the ears, creating a more symmetrical and natural appearance. This procedure is often chosen to reduce prominent ears or reshape ear cartilage, restoring harmony to facial proportions while maintaining a natural look.

010-070-0170

info@company.com

Blepharoplasty

Blepharoplasty rejuvenates the area around the eyes by removing excess skin, fat, or muscle from the upper and/or lower eyelids.

The result is a fresher, more youthful look that reflects how you truly feel — alert, confident, and full of life.
